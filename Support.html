Be Your Eyes Support

"Be Your Eyes" is an app that uses AI to help the visually impaired explore the world. It can recognize 1000+ common objects in life and text in multiple languages.

The App can quickly and accurately recognize more than 50 different texts and broadcast them in voice form;

It can monitor the distance of obstacles ahead in real time. If the distance is too close, it will send out an audible alarm and vibration alarm. The user can customize whether to turn it on;
